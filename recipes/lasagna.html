Mom's lasagna recipe

This is a delicious recipe for my mom's homade lasagna featuring fresh ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Ground Beef
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Ricotta Cheese

Instructions

  1. Boil noodles
  2. Brown ground beef
  3. Put layer of sauce down in pan
  4. Form layer of noodles, beef, ricotta, and sauce and repeat 6 times
  5. Bake in oven preheated to 450 for 80 minutes
  6. Top with mozarella for serving
